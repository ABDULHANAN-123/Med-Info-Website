WELLPAR WELLPAR 500 mg is a brand of paracetamol (also known as acetaminophen), which is widely used for its analgesic (pain-relieving) and antipyretic (fever-reducing) properties. Below is detailed information about the medicine: Composition Active Ingredient: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) 500 mg Indications WELLPAR 500 mg is used to relieve mild to moderate pain and reduce fever. Common conditions treated include: Pain Relief: Headaches Toothache Muscular pain Back pain Osteoarthritis Menstrual cramps Fever Reduction: Fever associated with colds, flu, or other infections Mechanism of Action Paracetamol works by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins in the brain, chemicals responsible for pain and fever. It acts centrally (in the brain) to reduce pain and fever, unlike NSAIDs which act peripherally. Dosage and Administration Adults and Children over 12 years: Dose: 500–1000 mg (1–2 tablets) every 4–6 hours as needed. Maximum Daily Dose: Do not exceed 4000 mg (8 tablets) in 24 hours. Children (6–12 years): Dose: 250–500 mg every 4–6 hours as needed. Maximum Daily Dose: Not more than 60 mg/kg/day in divided doses. Note: Always follow the prescribing doctor’s recommendations. Contraindications Allergy or hypersensitivity to paracetamol. Severe liver disease. Side Effects Paracetamol is generally well-tolerated but can cause the following rare side effects: Mild: Nausea Rash or itching Severe (Rare): Liver damage (in cases of overdose or prolonged use) Allergic reactions (e.g., swelling, difficulty breathing) Precautions Avoid alcohol consumption during treatment to reduce liver strain. Patients with liver or kidney problems should consult a doctor before use. Do not exceed the recommended dose to avoid toxicity. Overdose Symptoms of overdose include nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, sweating, and confusion. Overdose can cause severe liver damage and may be life-threatening. Immediate medical attention is required in case of overdose. Storage Store below 30°C in a dry place. Keep out of reach of children. Additional Information WELLPAR 500 mg is available as an over-the-counter (OTC) medication but should be used responsibly. If symptoms persist or worsen, seek medical advice.
